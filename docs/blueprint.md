# **App Name**: AgriAssist

## Core Features:

- Smart Diagnosis: AI-driven pest and disease identification tool that assists farmers in diagnosing common issues based on uploaded images or descriptions.
- Weather Insights: Localized weather forecasts specific to the farmer's region, including temperature, rainfall, and wind conditions.
- Crop Pricing: A simple interface to browse market data to determine which crops are likely to get the best prices.
- Smart notifications: Dashboard providing a quick overview of weather conditions and possible impacts.
- Resource Directory: A searchable directory of local agricultural resources and suppliers.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Earthy green (#6B8E23) to convey a sense of nature and growth.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC) to provide a neutral, calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Warm gold (#D4A27A) to highlight important elements and calls to action, complementing the green.
- Clean and readable sans-serif fonts to ensure clear presentation of information.
- Use of clear, representative icons related to weather, crops, and resources for easy navigation.
- Intuitive and organized layout with clear visual hierarchy for easy access to key information.
